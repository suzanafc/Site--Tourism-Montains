Tre Cime di Lavaredo

Elevation:2999M . First Recorded Ascent:1869
Italy

Tourism

Numerous routes lead from the surrounding communities to and around the peaks.The most common route is from Paternkofel/Monte Paterno to the alpine hut Auronzo at 2,333 m (7,654 ft), over Paternsattel(Patern Pass) to the alpine hut Dreizinnenhütte/Locatelli at 2,405 m (7,890 ft), and then to the peaks. There are a number of other routes as well. Since the front line between Italy and Austria during World Wad I ran through these mountains, there are a number of fortifications, man-made caves, and commemorative plaques in the area. Nearby communities inlude Auronzo di Cadore (in the province of Belluno, region of Veneto), Toblach/Dobbiaco, Sexten/Sesto, and the Puster Valley (in the province of Bolzano, region of Trentino-Alto Adige/Südtirol).
